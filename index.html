What makes it different

Six activity types.
All evaluated. All scored.

Quiz with feedback

Distractors based on real misconceptions — not obviously wrong. Students learn from every wrong answer.

Term matching

Connect terms to definitions by dragging. Points awarded for each correct pair.

Drag to sort

Order steps, events, or concepts correctly. Ideal for processes and timelines.

Flip cards with scoring

Students read the term, predict the definition, then reveal it and self-assess. Earns points on first try.

Fill in the blank

Key vocabulary placed in context. Word bank available for younger grades; open text for older students.

Score and report card

Every activity contributes to a final score. Students share results via WhatsApp, email, or print.
